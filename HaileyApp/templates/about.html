Our Project

Team of Bioinformatics students that developed a textmining application for Bitter Gourd.
This application pulls data form the PubMed database.
Article search was preformed only on the abstract and title.
Scoring for articles are as follows:
- Both disease and compound keywords found in the title: 10 points.
- Both disease and compound keywords found in abstract: 5 points.
- Disease in title and compound in abstract: 4 points.
- Compound in title and disease in abstract: 4 points
